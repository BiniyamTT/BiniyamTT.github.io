Visualizing Ethiopian Coffee Commodity Market

Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Tableau

Client:

Strata Market Intelligence

Problem:

ECX being the only commodities exchange in Ethiopia generates a ton of market data but lacks a solution in disseminating the collected information in an accessible and easy to understand way that stakeholders, both internally and externally, can use it to make better informed decisions.

Solution:

Provide visual representations to help people analyze, understand and extract meaningful information from large collections of data using a number of techniques such as:

  • Interactively letting the user drill down in the dataset to access the information at increasing levels of detail,
  • Using coordinated views to show different representations of the same data, or to show selected subsets of the data while retaining overview.
